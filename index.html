About Me

Easily the most interesting thing about me is my 3-legged Yorkshire terrier named Stumpy. Here he is dressed as a pirate for halloween, complete with peg leg. But do not let his looks fool you; he can be a mean SOB. Typical terrier, but he is the best!

Besides my best friend, I am known for being a CPA that jumped ship and took the dive into the startup world. Over the last 6 years, my founders and I have grown www.fullscript.com to become one of the fastest growing companies in Ottawa. Having completed a merger with a US company, we now have a team of 230+ talented staff and over $10M in monthly recurring revenue.

But despite this size, we continue to be a true start-up, never standing still and always looking for better ways to do things. Case in point, after 6 years of running operations and finance, and constantly struggling to find talent that live at the intersection of software engineering and business processes, I have decided to roll up my sleeves and be that person.

As I have yet to create a portfolio of any kind, I have decided to treat you to pictures of other cute dogs.
